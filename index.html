Alex Utts comes from a background in glass art, but is interested in expanding into 3d design and animation. Alex wants to use these tools for both 3d printing and for creating visuals that connect to music. He is also drawn to interactive art installations, because he likes the idea of people being able to step inside an environment and feel something beyond their normal day-to-day life.

The feelings he wants his work to bring out are inspiration, wonder, and even a kind of hypnotic escape.
