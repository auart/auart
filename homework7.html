Homework 7

For this project I illustrated a Japanese pagoda using a triad palette of blue, red, and green, building the scene with custom shapes and gradients to create depth. I used separate layers for the foreground trees, the pagoda, and the distant mountains, and avoided Image Trace to keep everything hand-built. I also referenced a separate image of the sōrin, the decorative spire on top of the pagoda. A fun fact is that the sōrin often sits on the same central pillar that helps pagodas sway safely during earthquakes. Including it helped keep the final illustration accurate while still fitting the simplified style.
